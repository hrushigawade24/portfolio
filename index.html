About Me

As a Blockchain Developer, I am dedicated to harnessing the transformative power of blockchain technology to drive innovation and solve complex challenges. With extensive experience in both public and private blockchain environments, I specialize in Ethereum, where I develop robust smart contracts and build decentralized applications (DApps). Additionally, I have practical expertise in chaincode creation with Hyperledger Fabric, enabling secure, permissioned blockchain networks for enterprise solutions.


My technical toolkit includes proficiency in languages such as Java, JavaScript, Solidity, and Go, along with a solid foundation in backend development using the MERN stack and CouchDB. I am well-versed in API development, RESTful service architecture, and tools like Postman, allowing me to create seamless integrations and scalable solutions for diverse applications.

I am always excited to connect with forward-thinking professionals and organizations to explore opportunities where blockchain can make a meaningful impact. Let’s collaborate to bring blockchain’s potential to life!
